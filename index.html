Everything guests need to know.

Hours, dining options, dietary care, and ways to reach us before you arrive.

Dine-in or Takeout

Reserve a table online or call ahead for pickup. Walk-ins welcomed for bar seating when available.

  • Service hours: Tue–Sun, 5:30–11:30 PM
  • Last kitchen orders at 10:45 PM
  • Takeout ready in 20–30 minutes

Dietary Requests

We cook to your needs. Let us know about allergies or preferences when booking or ordering.

  • Gluten-free and vegetarian swaps available
  • Allergen matrix on request
  • Kid-friendly options after 6 PM

Arrival Details

Located at 214 Lantern Street, District 7. Allow a few minutes for parking or rideshare drop-off.

  • Street and garage parking nearby
  • Ride share drop on Lantern and 3rd
  • Wheelchair accessible entrance

Payments & Contact

Pay securely and stay in touch. We reply quickly during service hours.

  • Cards, contactless, and gift cards accepted
  • Groups of 6+ may see 20% gratuity
  • Email hello@maisonlumiere.com or call (415) 555-0147
